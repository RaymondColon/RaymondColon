ENGINE TEST SHIELD

Designer & Fabricator · March 2025

Designed and fabricated a plexiglass airflow shield for the diesel engine test chamber after identifying that ambient airflow was interfering with the suspended fuel container, corrupting LabVIEW data readings. Worked from hand schematics using shop machinery, under time and material constraints. The shield eliminated fuel container oscillation and restored data reliability.
